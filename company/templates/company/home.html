About AgroFace

AGROFACE: Transforming Agriculture Value Chains in Africa.

AGROFACE is an innovative agriculture value chain platform that connects farmers and agribusinesses to markets, finance, and other value chain actors. The platform aims to enhance the competitiveness of small-scale farmers in Africa by providing access to improved inputs, technologies, and best practices to increase productivity and income.
