We are Taking Small Steps to Make

Earth Better Planet

AGROFACE is an innovative agriculture value chain platform that connects farmers and agribusinesses to markets, finance, and other value chain actors. The platform aims to enhance the competitiveness of small-scale farmers in Africa by providing access to improved inputs, technologies, and best practices to increase productivity and income. AGROFACE provides a range of services, including market linkage, finance, and training. Farmers can access information on market demand, prices, and quality standards through the platform and connect directly with buyers, reducing the role of intermediaries and increasing profits. The platform also offers financial services such as loans, savings, and insurance to help farmers manage risks and invest in their operations. In conclusion, AGROFACE is an innovative agriculture value chain platform that is transforming the way small-scale farmers in Africa connect to markets and finance. By providing access to improved inputs, technologies, and best practices, AGROFACE is improving productivity and income for farmers and creating a more efficient and sustainable agriculture value chain.
